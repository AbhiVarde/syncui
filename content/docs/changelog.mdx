#### ⏰ Nov '25 – Time Pickers Added

**New Component:**  
→ [Time Pickers](/docs/timepickers) – Seamless time selection with **12-Hour**, **24-Hour**, **With Seconds**, and **Presets** variants. Features smooth animations, dark mode support.

#### 📅 Oct '25 – Date Picker Variants Added

**New Component:**  
→ [Date Pickers](/docs/datepickers) – Flexible date selection with **Single**, **Range**, **Presets**, and **With Time** variants. Includes smooth animations, dark mode support, and smart preset options like Today, Yesterday, Last 7 Days, and more.

#### 🪄 Sep '25 – Component Search & Smart Autocomplete

**New Feature:**  
→ **Component Search** – Instantly find any component from the top header and jump straight to its docs. Say goodbye to endless scrolling! 🚀

**New Component:**  
→ [Autocompletes](/docs/autocompletes) – Smart, fast, and accessible input suggestions with smooth animations and flexible variants.

#### 📋 Aug '25 – Fresh Components & OTP Update

**New Component:**  
→ [Adaptive Forms](/docs/forms) – Multi-step, Login, Register & Contact variants, smoothly animated and fully responsive.

**Component Update:**  
→ [TextFields](/docs/textfields) – New OTP variant added with 6-digit verification, auto-focus navigation, and responsive design.
